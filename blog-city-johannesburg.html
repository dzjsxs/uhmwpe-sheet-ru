Johannesburg, the economic capital of South Africa and the hub of the Witwatersrand gold mining region, sits at the centre of Africa's most sophisticated mining and industrial economy. The Gauteng province and surrounding areas host the operations of Anglo American, Gold Fields, Sibanye-Stillwater, Impala Platinum, and dozens of other major mining companies. South Africa's unique combination of deep-level gold mining, platinum group metals (PGM) production, and extensive coal operations creates a diverse and demanding market for high-performance engineering plastics.

UHMWPE for South African Deep-Level Gold Mining

South Africa's deep-level gold mines — some extending more than 4km below surface — present extreme engineering challenges including high rock temperatures, seismic activity, and the handling of very fine, abrasive gold-bearing ore. UHMWPE wear liners are used extensively in underground ore passes, loading pockets, and surface processing plants at operations including AngloGold Ashanti's Mponeng mine and Harmony Gold's operations in the Free State. Our UHMWPE products are available in antistatic grades (surface resistivity < 10⁶ Ω) for underground applications where electrostatic discharge is a safety concern.

Polyurethane Screens for Platinum and Chrome Processing

The Bushveld Igneous Complex — the world's largest platinum group metals deposit, stretching from Rustenburg through Limpopo — requires extensive screening equipment for PGM ore classification and chrome recovery. Our polyurethane screen panels are engineered for the specific gravity and abrasivity of Bushveld ores, with aperture sizes from 0.5mm to 100mm and panel dimensions to suit all major screen frame systems used by Impala Platinum, Northam Platinum, and Samancor Chrome.

HDPE Systems for South African Infrastructure

South Africa's ongoing infrastructure development — including Transnet's port and rail expansion, Eskom's power station maintenance, and municipal water infrastructure upgrades — creates sustained demand for HDPE pipe, sheet, and fabricated components. Our HDPE products are manufactured to SANS 966 (equivalent to ISO 4427) specifications and are available in PE80 and PE100 grades for pressure pipe applications.

South African Standards (SANS)

Our products for the South African market comply with relevant SANS (South African National Standards) and international ISO/ASTM equivalents. For mining applications, products are available with SABS (South African Bureau of Standards) test certificates. Our HDPE pipe-grade materials meet SANS 966 specifications.
